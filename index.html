I am Cami Pacifici and I am a senior data scientist for astronomy at the Space Telescope Science Institute. I am the Product Owner of a group of software engineers to develop the JWST data analysis and visualization tools, Jdaviz. We also collaborate on the development of Astropy coordinated packages (photutils, specutils, specreduce).

Research wise, I am interested in galaxy formation and evolution and especially into deriving the star formation histories of galaxies from the fossil records in the data. I am survey manager of the GARDEN and MEADOW surveys where we use the NIRSpec multi object spectrograph on JWST to look at galaxies at redshifts 1 to 6 and derive their physical and kinematic properties.

JWST work

The JWST Data Analysis Tools

I am the Product Owners of the JWST Data Analysis Tools at the Space Telescope Science Institute. These include the development of astronomical libraries (Specutils, Photutils, Specreduce, etc.), the JWST Data Analysis and Visualization package (Jdaviz), and the development of science-case Jupyter Notebooks (hosted under the jdat_notebooks repository).

The JWebbinars

From 2020 to 2022, I was in charge of organizing and running the JWebbinars, which are dedicated webinars about JWST and how to use the data. Material and videos from past webinars are available at the JWebbinar webpage.
